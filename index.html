What is kashmiriyat?

kashmiriyat is an intitiative by a duo of students currently studying at Delhi Public School Srinagar(visit).We aim to spread awareness about the Kashmiri culture and heritage by sharing information that is otherwise not available to common folk. You can learn more about our mission in about us section.

How can you contribute?

You can send any useful information on email or DM us on our social media handles, if you are interested you can even write articles for us and we'll publish them.
